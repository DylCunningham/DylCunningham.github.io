Our family has been fortunate enough to be able to take several really great vacations over the years. One of our favorite places to go to is Panama City Beach®. At PCB, we love to play an interactive game called EyeSpy®. Below are lists of locations we've been to and ones we still plan on going to someday.

  • EyeSpy Locations we've been to:
    1. Panama City Beach, FL
    2. St. Louis, MO
    3. Anaheim, CA
    4. Cincinatti, OH
    5. Kansas City, KS
    6. Wisconsin Dells, WI

  • EyeSpy Locations we'd like to visit:
    1. Grapevine, TX
    2. Niagra Falls, NY
    3. Chicago, IL
    4. Atlana, GA
    5. Portland, OR
